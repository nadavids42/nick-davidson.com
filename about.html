About Me

I’m Nick Davidson — a builder, problem-solver, and lifelong learner. My background blends physics, data analytics, and hands-on robotics, but what really drives me is creating things that work and sharing that process with others.

These days, you’ll find me tinkering with robotics kits, building data visualizations for fun, or teaching kids how to wire their first circuits. I’m always chasing ideas — whether it’s a new way to automate a home project or explore a dataset that sparks my curiosity. For me, learning is hands-on, and the best way to understand something is to build it.
